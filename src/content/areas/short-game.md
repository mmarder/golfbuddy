## Clubs

- **56° wedge** — everything inside about 80 yards: pitches, chips around the green, and bunker shots. Your scoring club.

## Setup

Short-game stance (differs from the full swing):

- Feet closer together.
- Left foot set back a bit, behind the hips.
- Ball centred between both feet.

## Lesson learnings

- **Chipping** — land the ball on a spot near the green and let it roll out to the hole.
- **Pitching** — higher, softer shots from around 40–70 yd. Control distance with the length of your backswing, not by swinging harder.
- **Bunker** — hit the sand a couple of inches behind the ball, not the ball itself; the sand splashes it out. Commit to the swing — don't quit on it.
